Field Notes — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

The short version: We only collect what's needed for the App to work. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, we don't track you, and we don't use your content to train anything. Your notes are stored securely and you can delete them whenever you want.

1. Who We Are

FICSIT Field Notes is developed by Lueken Good Design LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. What We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Data When Why
Email address At sign-in Identify your account for sync
Display name At sign-in Display in-app (optional)
Google user ID At sign-in Unique account key
Session tasks, needs, factories, scratch notes When you create them Your personal content, stored per user
Game settings (belt tier, alternate recipes, etc.) When you change them Personalize planner output

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect device location or contacts
  • We do not collect analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking
  • We do not use any third-party ad networks
  • We do not collect device identifiers beyond what Google Sign-In requires
  • We do not read or store any data from other apps on your device

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate your account via Google Sign-In
  • Sync your notes and settings across your devices
  • Load and display your personal content within the App

That's the entire list. We do not sell data, share it with third parties for marketing, use it to train machine learning models, or analyze it in aggregate for commercial insights.

4. Where Your Data is Stored

Your account and notes are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (railway.com), a cloud infrastructure provider. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with access controls limiting it to the App's backend service. Railway's own privacy practices are available at railway.com/legal/privacy.

A local copy of your notes is also cached on your device (via Android's SharedPreferences or iOS equivalent) so the App works offline. This local data is removed when you uninstall the App or sign out.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In to authenticate users. When you sign in, Google shares your email, name, and user ID with us as described above. Google's own privacy policy applies to their collection: policies.google.com/privacy.

5.2 Satisfactory Wiki

The App's Wiki tab embeds content from satisfactory.wiki.gg, a community-maintained wiki. When you use the Wiki tab, the embedded browser may make requests directly to that site. We don't control what data is collected by the wiki itself.

5.3 Railway

Our backend runs on Railway. Railway may collect server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of its infrastructure services. We don't have access to these logs beyond standard operational needs.

5.4 Donation Platforms

If you choose to donate via Ko-Fi, Ko-Fi's own privacy policy governs that transaction (ko-fi.com/Manage/privacy). We do not receive your personal information from Ko-Fi beyond the donation itself. Bitcoin donations are pseudonymous by nature; we receive only the wallet transaction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. If you want your data deleted, email systems@lueken-good.design from the email address associated with your account and we'll remove your records within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of what we store about you
  • Correction — fix inaccurate data (most of this you can do in-app)
  • Deletion — request removal of your data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, email systems@lueken-good.design. We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we'll delete it.

9. Security

We use HTTPS for all data transmission and rely on Railway's infrastructure security for data at rest. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password on your Google account and to sign out on shared devices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we'll try to notify you in-app.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email systems@lueken-good.design.
